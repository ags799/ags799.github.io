# Multiline Cursor
1. Start in normal mode.
1. Place your cursor with `h`, `j`, `k`, `l`.
1. Press `Ctrl-v`.
1. Highlight a chunk of lines with `j` and `k`.
1. Press `Shift-i`.
1. Enter your character sequence.
1. Press `Escape`.
1. Note that the characters have added to multiple lines.
